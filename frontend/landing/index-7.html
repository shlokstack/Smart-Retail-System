FAQ

Frequently asked questions

In case you missed anything and had some more questions.

How does ShelfPulse AI detect empty shelves?

ShelfPulse AI uses computer vision to analyze live camera feeds and identify missing or misplaced products in real time. The system continuously monitors shelves and instantly flags stockouts without manual checks.

Do we need special cameras or hardware?

No. ShelfPulse AI works with existing CCTV or security cameras already installed in your store. This makes deployment fast, cost-effective, and scalable.

How accurate is the system?

ShelfPulse AI is designed for high-density retail environments and delivers reliable detection accuracy using advanced object detection models. Accuracy improves over time as the system learns from store data.

Can ShelfPulse AI predict when products will run out?

Yes. The system analyzes historical sales, demand trends, promotions, and external factors to forecast when products are likely to go out of stock, helping teams restock proactively.

What happens when a product goes out of stock?

ShelfPulse AI immediately sends alerts to store managers or staff through notifications, dashboards, or email, enabling quick restocking and preventing lost sales.

Can this system scale to multiple stores?

Yes. ShelfPulse AI is built with scalable architecture that can monitor multiple stores and camera feeds from a single centralized dashboard.

How does ShelfPulse AI help increase revenue?

By detecting stockouts early and ensuring shelves remain stocked, ShelfPulse AI prevents missed sales opportunities and improves product availability, directly increasing store revenue.
